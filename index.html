About Us

PathFinder is a platform that connects individuals with expert mentors who were once users of the platform and have since become the succesful and reknowned in their respective fields. Users can explore different career fields, receive industry insights, schedule one-on-one consultations, and access career advice. Whether you're starting out or making a career change, PathFinder provides guidance from those who have walked the same path and achieved success.
